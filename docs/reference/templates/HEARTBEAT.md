# HEARTBEAT.md

## Periodic Checks (run 2-4x daily, rotate through)

- **Emails** — Any urgent unread messages?
- **Calendar** — Events in the next 24-48h?
- **Leads** — Any new leads to review in `leads/`?

## Memory Hygiene (weekly, during low-activity heartbeat)

- Skim recent daily logs — anything worth adding to `MEMORY.md`?
- Leads older than 30 days in `leads/` — clean up

## When to Reply vs HEARTBEAT_OK

**Reply when:**
- Urgent email arrived
- Calendar event under 2 hours away
- Lead requires a human decision
- It has been more than 8 hours since the last message

**Stay quiet (HEARTBEAT_OK) when:**
- Late night (23:00-08:00) unless urgent
- Nothing new since last check
- You checked less than 30 minutes ago
